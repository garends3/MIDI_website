Christos Kanakis

Brief Description

Medical imaging researcher, specializing in the development and validation of diffusion MRI techniques for improved diagnostic accuracy. Currently a second-year PhD candidate at UMC Utrecht, working in collaboration with Cerebriu A/S (Copenhagen) on developing an automated preprocessing, quality control and detection MRI pipeline for prostate cancer.
