ISMRM conference 2026 in Cape Town

Recap of highlight ISMRM conference 2026 in Cape Town.

We are pleased to announce that Dr. Tatiana Nikolaeva's abstract, "High-Frequency OGSE at 28.2T in a Cortical Brain Organoid and Fiber Phantom", has been nominated for the Best Power Pitch Award. This recognition highlights the innovative work being carried out by members of the diffusion MRI community and showcases the impact of cutting-edge research in the field.

Looking ahead, we are excited to introduce the upcoming Microstructure Challenge, which will provide a platform for the community to evaluate and compare emerging approaches to correlate histology to MRI microscopy. Data will be provided with our unique imaging capabilities at 28.2T field. Further details will be presented during the Microstructure Imaging Workshop at the ESMRMB Conference in Girona. We encourage all interested researchers to stay tuned for announcements and opportunities to participate.
